# Sample Before/After Review Replies

These examples are anonymized public-style situations.

## Example 1: late service window

**Review:** “Waited all afternoon and nobody called until after the appointment window. Wasted my day.”

**Weak owner reply:** “We were busy and short staffed. You should have called dispatch.”

**Saved reply:** “Hi Jordan, I’m sorry we left you waiting and did not communicate sooner. That is frustrating and not the standard we want. We’re reviewing the scheduling handoff so this does not repeat. If you’re open to it, please contact Sam at hello@example.com so we can look at the appointment details and try to make this right.”

## Example 2: quality concern

**Review:** “The job looked rushed and I had to redo part of it myself.”

**Weak owner reply:** “This is unfair. We did what you asked and you never complained.”

**Saved reply:** “Hi Taylor, I’m sorry the finished work did not meet expectations. We want customers to feel confident in the job, and it sounds like we missed that mark. Please contact Sam at hello@example.com with the service date so we can review what happened and see what we can reasonably correct.”

## Example 3: pricing confusion

**Review:** “Quoted one thing then charged more. Never using them again.”

**Weak owner reply:** “You added extra work. Read your invoice.”

**Saved reply:** “Hi Morgan, I’m sorry the final price felt unclear. We try to make scope changes easy to understand, and it sounds like we could have explained this better. Please reach Sam at hello@example.com so we can review the quote and invoice with you directly.”
