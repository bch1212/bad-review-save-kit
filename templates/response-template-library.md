# Review Response Template Library

Use these as drafts only. The owner should confirm facts before posting publicly.

## 1) Service delay / missed expectation

Hi {{reviewer_name}}, thank you for taking the time to share this. I’m sorry the timing and communication did not meet the standard we aim for. We’re reviewing what happened on our side so we can tighten the process. If you’re open to it, please contact {{contact_method}} and ask for {{owner_name}} so we can understand the details and try to make this right.

## 2) Quality complaint

Hi {{reviewer_name}}, I’m sorry the finished work did not feel right to you. That is not the experience we want customers to have. We’d appreciate the chance to look at the details and see what we can reasonably correct. Please reach {{owner_name}} at {{contact_method}} with your job/date details.

## 3) Staff behavior complaint

Hi {{reviewer_name}}, thank you for flagging this. Courtesy matters to us, and I’m sorry your interaction felt frustrating. We’re addressing this internally and would like to learn more so we can improve. Please contact {{owner_name}} at {{contact_method}} if you’re willing to share details.

## 4) Price / surprise fee complaint

Hi {{reviewer_name}}, I’m sorry the pricing or scope was not clear enough. We try to be upfront, and it sounds like we missed an opportunity to explain things better. Please reach out to {{contact_method}} so we can review the invoice/scope and answer any questions.

## 5) Unverified / possible wrong business

Hi {{reviewer_name}}, we take feedback seriously, but we can’t match this to a recent customer record from the details provided. If this was for our business, please contact {{owner_name}} at {{contact_method}} with the service date or invoice name so we can investigate and respond properly.

## 6) Already resolved privately

Hi {{reviewer_name}}, thank you for speaking with us directly. I’m glad we were able to discuss the situation and work toward a resolution. We appreciate the feedback and are using it to improve our process.

## 7) Angry review where facts are disputed

Hi {{reviewer_name}}, I’m sorry this experience was frustrating. We see some details differently, but this public thread is not the best place to debate specifics. We’d rather review the facts directly and look for a fair next step. Please contact {{owner_name}} at {{contact_method}}.

## 8) Short generic negative review

Hi {{reviewer_name}}, I’m sorry to see this rating. We’d like to understand what happened and see if there’s a way to improve the experience. Please reach {{owner_name}} at {{contact_method}} with any details you’re comfortable sharing.

## Owner reply rules

1. Thank them for the feedback.
2. Acknowledge the issue without arguing.
3. Avoid private details, blame, sarcasm, accusations, or long explanations.
4. Move specifics to a private channel.
5. Say what will improve, not why the customer is wrong.
6. Never offer incentives to change/remove reviews.
